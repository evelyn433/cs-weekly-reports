Four detractors this week (vs 3 last week), all with rating 1: one on WhatsApp Support and three on WhatsApp Collections. Two left comments — one about Collections message timing ("horários muito cedo") and another expressing general disappointment. CrediPay email continues with very low survey engagement — 0 responses from 24 surveys sent this week, despite the channel still producing tickets.

🔧 Operations Insights & Follow-ups

Items 1 and 2 are improvement recommendations identified this week. Items 3 onward are tickets and issues raised in the #general-support channel during the week of 31/05 to 06/06, ordered chronologically.

1. Validate contact unlink functionality (phone / email per CNPJ)

It would be valuable to verify whether the functionality to unlink a phone number or email from a given CNPJ is working correctly. We have indications that numbers we had marked to be unlinked are still receiving messages. Additionally, we should validate whether new contacts being registered are being properly saved and used by the system for outbound communications.

⚠️ This affects the integrity of customer-facing communications: contacts who were intentionally unlinked should stop receiving messages, and newly registered contacts should start receiving them. Worth a quick technical audit to confirm the current behavior end-to-end.

2. Adjust Aira behavior outside business hours and on holidays

It would be helpful to adjust the messages Aira sends on weekends and Friday from 18h onward. When Aira needs to escalate to human support during these periods, it would be better if she instructed the customer to reach out again on Monday from 9h. There is no operation on weekends, and depending on the timestamp of ticket creation, if more than 24 hours have elapsed we are no longer able to respond by Monday because the response window has closed.

Also, on holidays or any events where there is no operation, Aira should ideally send an informational message setting the right expectation about when the customer will be attended.

⚠️ This directly affects CSAT: ticket #33911 this week is an example — boleto request received on Saturday 06/06, required internal validation, customer was only attended on Monday 08/06, and rated 1.
