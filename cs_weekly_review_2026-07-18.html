📋 Executive Summary

A steady week on higher volume. Total tickets rose to 1,050 (+15.4% vs 910) — the highest we have measured — with every channel up: WhatsApp Support 148 (from 145), the widget 49 (from 34), email 55 (from 39), and WhatsApp Collections 798 (from 692).

On Customer Support performance (over the 252 Support tickets, excluding Collections), the team held the line despite the higher load. SLA stayed just above target at 90.5% (−0.2 pp), with average net business minutes steady at 56.9 minutes (from 58.0). Deflection rose to 52.8% (+4.2 pp) — the best reading in several weeks, though still below the 70% target. CSAT was 92.9% (26 of 28 positive), just under the 95% target — but one of the two negatives (#39173, "Gostei atendimento") is a clear mis-tap; excluding it puts CSAT at ~96%. Response rate was 11.1% (28 answers from 252 surveys).

On Collections, CSAT rebounded to 89.5% (+13.0 pp from 76.5%, 17 of 19 positive), with response rate essentially flat at 2.4% (19 of 798).

Four detractors this week (2 Support, 2 Collections), ratings 1–2, all buyers. Two are clear mis-taps with positive comments ("Boa", "Gostei atendimento"); the one genuine Support detractor (#39469) wrote "não consegui contato" (couldn't reach us). CrediPay email captured 2 survey responses this week (both positive).

Channel observations

CrediPay Support (widget) remained the strongest response channel — 24.5% response rate (12 of 49), all 12 positive — with volume recovering to 49.
