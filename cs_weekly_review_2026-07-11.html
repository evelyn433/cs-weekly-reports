📋 Executive Summary

A strong recovery week for Support after the rough prior week. Total volume eased to 910 (−9.1% vs 1,001), down across every channel: WhatsApp Support 145 (from 179), the widget 34 (from 48), email 39 (from 64), and WhatsApp Collections 692 (from 710). Note that Thursday July 09 was a holiday with no operation — buyer inbound continued via the bot but there was no rep activity that day.

On Customer Support performance (over the 218 Support tickets, excluding Collections), every KPI moved back in the right direction. CSAT returned to 100.0% (21 of 21 answered positive, zero Support detractors) — up from 91.7% and back above the 95% target. SLA recovered to 91.9% (+7.3 pp), back above the 90% target, and average net business minutes more than halved to 42.7 minutes (from 108.6). Deflection improved to 48.2% (+6.6 pp), still below the 70% target. CSAT response rate was 9.6% (21 answers from 218 surveys).

On Collections, CSAT dropped to 76.5% (−9.2 pp, 13 of 17 answered positive) — but on a very small sample of 17 answers, so a few negatives move it a lot. Response rate more than doubled off a low base to 2.5% (17 of 692).

All four detractors this week were on WhatsApp Collections (zero on Support), ratings 1–3, all buyers. Only one carried a comment (#38448): a buyer noting they were charged two days before the due date — a process observation rather than a service failure. CrediPay email again returned 0 survey responses.
