📋 Executive Summary

This was a strong recovery week after the intensity of the previous one. All three Support KPIs bounced back significantly: CSAT recovered to 97.1% (+6.4 pp, above the 95% target), SLA recovered to 93.9% (+10.2 pp, above the 90% target), and average net business minutes more than halved from 77.2 to 32.5 minutes. Deflection stayed roughly flat at 27.3% (−1.8 pp), still the only KPI consistently below target.

Total ticket volume was 1,044 tickets (Hex), down from 1,103 the previous week (−59, −5.3%). Of note: WhatsApp Collections dropped 7.5% (766 vs 828) and CrediPay (email) grew 37% (85 vs 62), the standout movement in Support channels.

On Customer Support performance (calculated only over the 278 Support tickets — WhatsApp Support, CrediPay Support widget and CrediPay email — excluding Collections), CSAT response rate held at 12.4% (34 answers) vs 11.6%, slightly up. The CrediPay Support widget continues to lead engagement with a 33.3% response rate, far above the other channels.

On Collections, CSAT jumped to 90.5% (vs 80.8%, +9.7 pp) — a meaningful improvement, although the response rate fell from 5.5% to 4.5%. The l2_noise_or_spam volume remained elevated (221 tickets, ~29% of Collections volume), consistent with the prior week.

Only 3 detractors this week (vs 8 last week), all with rating 3 — one in CrediPay widget ("Não aprovou faturamento") and two in WhatsApp Collections (both consultations of boletos). No detractors with rating 1 or 2 — a significant qualitative improvement.
